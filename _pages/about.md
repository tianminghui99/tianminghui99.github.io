Welcome! I am Minghui Tian (田明慧), currently a Ph.D. student in the School of Software at Shandong University, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Liqiang Nie and Prof. Xuemeng Song. Since 2026, I have also been a visiting student at the Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech). Prior to this, I received my Bachelor's degree from the School of Computer Science and Technology at Shandong University in 2022. My research interests primarily lie in multimedia computing and information retrieval.
